The IDR logo uses a cube‑inspired geometric icon to symbolize structure, resilience, and the layered nature of digital risk. The primary palette of orange, black, and white balances innovation with authority while ensuring strong contrast and legibility. Bold sans‑serif typography reinforces a modern, tech‑driven educational identity. Two variants are provided — icon‑only for small applications and icon + text for full branding — both designed to remain clear and scalable across digital and print contexts.
