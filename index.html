About IDR

Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We combine academic insight through UK university partnerships with real-world projects to prepare practitioners for critical sectors such as financial services and critical infrastructure.
